# Archived Reviews

Historical daily, weekly, and quarterly reviews.

## What Goes Here

Reviews automatically archive here:
- **Daily reviews** — Created by `/daily-review`
- **Weekly reviews** — Created by `/week-review`
- **Quarterly reviews** — Created by `/quarter-review`

## Naming Convention

- Daily: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- Weekly: `YYYY-Wxx.md` (e.g., `2026-W04.md`)
- Quarterly: `YYYY-Qx.md` (e.g., `2026-Q1.md`)

## Purpose

Archived reviews are your learning record:
- Track accomplishments and growth over time
- Spot patterns and recurring challenges
- Prepare for performance reviews and promotions
- Understand how your focus evolves
- Extract learnings for `06-Resources/Learnings/`

## Retention

Keep indefinitely. Reviews are your historical record of progress and learning—critical for career development and quarterly planning.

## Integration

- Weekly reviews reference recent daily reviews
- Quarterly reviews aggregate weekly reviews
- Career evidence pulls from reviews
- `/career-coach` analyzes review patterns
