## Summary

Modernizing our customer self-service portal based on feedback that the current experience is outdated and hard to navigate. Major UX overhaul with improved dashboard and reporting.

---

## Objectives

1. Reduce support ticket volume by 30%
2. Improve portal NPS from 32 to 50+
3. Increase self-service task completion rate to 80%
