# Customer Portal v2.0 Launch

**Date:** October 2025
**Initiative:** Customer Portal Redesign
**Impact Level:** High
**Competencies:** Product Strategy, Execution Excellence, Customer Insight

---

## Context

Led complete redesign of TechCorp's customer portal to improve self-service capabilities and reduce support load. Previous portal had low adoption (35%) and high support ticket volume.

---

## What I Did

### Strategy & Discovery
- Conducted 25 customer interviews to understand pain points
- Analyzed support ticket data to identify top user issues
- Built business case: projected 40% reduction in support costs
- Defined success metrics and OKRs

### Execution
- Wrote comprehensive PRD with clear user stories
- Led weekly design reviews with Maya's team
- Partnered with Jordan on technical approach (API redesign required)
- Managed scope decisions to hit Q3 deadline
- Coordinated with Customer Success on rollout plan

### Launch & Iteration
- Phased rollout: beta (20 customers) → general availability
- Daily monitoring of adoption and support tickets
- Quick iteration based on early feedback (3 critical fixes in first week)
- Post-launch communication to customers and internal teams

---

## Results

**Adoption:**
- Self-service adoption: 35% → 55% (+20 percentage points)
- Active portal users: 2,500 → 6,200 (+148%)

**Support Impact:**
- Support tickets: -30% reduction
- Time to resolution: -25% improvement
- Customer satisfaction with portal: 4.2/5 (up from 2.8/5)

**Business Impact:**
- Projected annual savings: $450K in support costs
- Customer NPS improved by +8 points
- 15% reduction in churn for accounts using portal
