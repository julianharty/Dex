## Job Description

### Role Summary

Lead product strategy and execution for TechCorp's mobile and platform initiatives. Own the product roadmap from discovery through launch, working cross-functionally with Engineering, Design, Marketing, and Sales to deliver high-impact features that drive user growth and engagement.

### Key Responsibilities

1. **Product Strategy**
   - Define product vision and strategy for mobile platform
   - Conduct market research and competitive analysis
   - Identify opportunities through customer feedback and data analysis
   - Prioritize features based on business impact and customer value

2. **Cross-Functional Leadership**
   - Partner with Engineering Lead (Jordan Lee) on technical feasibility and roadmap
   - Collaborate with Design Lead (Maya Patel) on user experience
   - Work with Sales and Customer Success on customer insights
   - Present roadmap updates to executive leadership

3. **Execution & Delivery**
   - Write detailed product requirements documents (PRDs)
   - Manage product backlog and sprint planning
   - Make scope and timeline trade-off decisions
   - Drive projects from kickoff through launch and post-launch analysis

4. **Customer Research**
   - Conduct user interviews and gather feedback
   - Analyze usage data and identify patterns
   - Maintain relationships with key beta customers
   - Validate product hypotheses through testing

5. **Team Development**
   - Mentor junior PMs and product associates
   - Participate in hiring and onboarding
   - Share learnings and best practices across product team
