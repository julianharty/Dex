## Priority 3: Build Executive Presence

**Gap:** Good communicator with peers, but need to develop executive-level communication (concise, strategic, confident).

**Goal:** Communicate effectively with execs and board-level stakeholders. Develop strong written narratives.

**Success Metrics:**
- Deliver 2+ executive presentations with positive feedback
- Write 3+ strategy memos/decision docs
- Participate in 1 board or investor presentation
- Get specific feedback on exec communication improvement

**Key Actions:**
- [ ] Join Toastmasters or similar (public speaking practice)
- [ ] Practice exec presentations with manager (feedback loops)
- [ ] Study Jeff Bezos narrative memo style
- [ ] Write monthly strategy memo (practice written narratives)
- [ ] Shadow Sarah in exec meetings to observe communication style
- [ ] Request feedback from execs after presentations

**Evidence to Capture:**
- Executive presentation recordings/feedback
- Strategy memos written
- Board/investor presentation participation
- Quotes from execs about communication improvement
